ABOUT ME

Hello friends! I'm a current undergraduate student at Duke University with a love for photography and visual aesthetics, who also spends quite a bit of time at home in Hanover, New Hampshire. I'm double majoring in Electrical & Computer Engineering (ECE) and Visual Media Studies (VMS), but in my free time I love playing soccer, going on Outing Club trips, and sharing music. Check out my Spotify playlists for every hour of the day!

As a self-taught photographer, I shoot all kinds of subjects, but my favorite genres are travel photography and portraiture. Throughout the years, I've learned more through VMS classes, FORM Magazine at Duke of which I am the Travel & Culture team director, and other inspirational photographers. Whether you're here to browse or inquire about hiring, feel free to get in touch through the Contact page!

Mindy Wu, 2020
